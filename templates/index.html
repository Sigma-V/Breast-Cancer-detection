About

Welcome to our breast cancer detection tool. Our model employs logistic regression, a robust statistical method, to analyze 30 distinct parameters that are critical in diagnosing breast cancer. By evaluating these parameters, our model aims to provide accurate and early detection of breast cancer.

Through rigorous testing, our model has achieved a remarkable accuracy rate of 95% on test data, showcasing its effectiveness in the field of medical diagnostics.
